📜 Your Deed Poll Wording

“I of have given up my name [old name] and have adopted for all purposes the name .

“Where applicable, I have informed the police that I have given up my name [old name] and have adopted the name [new name] and understand that a failure to do so is a criminal offence.

“I also understand that when I apply for official documents in my new name, such as a British Passport, the relevant authorities may check the history and validity of my old name [old name] and new name [new name]. PRIVATE & CONFIDENTIAL

TO WHOM IT MAY CONCERN

Re: [patient's full name]  DOB: [date of birth]
[patient's address]

I am writing to confirm the following in support of the above named patient's application for a Gender Recognition Certificate:

Living in Affirmed Gender
The above named patient has been living in their affirmed gender for a period of at least two years.

Intention to Continue Living in Affirmed Gender
The above named patient has expressed a clear and committed intention to live in this gender for the rest of their life.

They use their specified name and gender for all purposes in their day-to-day life and within their medical records at this practice.

Please do not hesitate to contact me if you require any further information or clarification.

Yours faithfully,

GP signature

[Dr's name]
